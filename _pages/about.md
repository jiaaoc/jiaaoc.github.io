Hello, I'm Jiaao Chen!

I am a Founding Member of Technical Staff at [Eigen AI](https://www.eigen.ai/), where I lead the development of self-evolving multi-agent platforms for agentic AI systems. My work focuses on LLM post-training (SFT, GRPO/RL), tool-use agents, and multi-agent data pipelines.

Previously, I was a Research Scientist at [Meta](https://about.meta.com/) (GenAI team), where I worked on agentic post-training in TBD agent workstream, and an Applied Scientist at [Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/), working on LLM reasoning for e-commerce.

I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science from [Georgia Institute of Technology](https://www.gatech.edu/) / [Stanford University](https://www.stanford.edu/) in 2024, advised by Prof. [Diyi Yang](https://cs.stanford.edu/~diyiyang/). I have 20+ first-author publications at top venues including NeurIPS, ICLR, EMNLP, and ACL. [[Google Scholar](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=Pi9IVvUAAAAJ&hl=en): 5,000+ citations, h-index: 24, i10-index: 30]

Before Georgia Tech, I obtained my bachelor's degree from [Zhejiang University](http://www.zju.edu.cn/english/), where I was in the ACEE Honor Class at Chu Kochen Honors College (top 40 out of 6,000 students).

## Research Interests

- **LLM Agents & Tool Use:** Multi-agent platforms, function-calling, agentic data synthesis, and tool-use planning
- **LLM Post-Training:** Supervised fine-tuning (SFT), reinforcement learning (GRPO/PPO), reward modeling, and RLHF pipelines
- **LLM Reasoning:** Compositional reasoning, chain-of-thought, dynamic evaluation, and skill adaptation
- **Data-Efficient NLP:** Data augmentation, semi-supervised learning, and parameter-efficient fine-tuning
- **Language Generation:** Diffusion language models, dialogue summarization, and controllable generation
